🧩 Twisted Corner Detector

Got a 2×2 (Pocket Cube)? Use the 2×2 version →

Scan your Rubik's cube with your phone camera. This tool figures out whether a corner is twisted the wrong way — the reassembly bug that makes a cube impossible to solve — and tells you exactly which corner to twist and which way.

Before you start

• Use good, even lighting. Avoid glare on the stickers.

• You'll scan all 6 faces, one at a time, following the on-screen prompts.

• Colors are learned from your cube's center stickers, so it doesn't matter that your white stickers are black — it just calibrates to whatever it sees.

• After scanning you can tap any square to correct a mis-read color.
